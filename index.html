FAQ

Got Questions?

Is INNOSpark really 100% free for students?

Yes, completely free. We're funded by grants, partnerships, and sponsors — never by students. Every workshop, course, competition, and mentorship session is at zero cost.

Do I need prior business experience?

Absolutely not! INNOSpark is designed for complete beginners. All you need is curiosity and a willingness to learn. We start from zero and build up.

How do I become a mentor?

Apply through our mentor program! We welcome founders, VCs, industry professionals, and even senior students. You commit just 2-4 hours per month.

Can my school partner with INNOSpark?

Yes! Schools, universities, and community organizations can partner to bring our programs directly to their students. We provide all materials and training.

What happens after the pitch competition?

Winners get funding, incubation support, and continued mentorship. But even non-winners get feedback, connections, and access to our alumni network for ongoing support.
